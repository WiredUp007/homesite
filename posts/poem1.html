To Dead Ambitions

2025

And a tear falls down, On the pages creased and torn. And hands tighten, Around the photographs gray and worn. And lips tremble, Devoid of words and hopes long gone. A bitter smile comes, Mourning the loss of all what could have been, And for all that they were born,

And a tear falls down, As without a noise, the box is shut down. So long, So gone.

To all the dead artists, poets and writers, who are now hollow engineers, doctors and lawyers.
