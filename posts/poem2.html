2025

And honey,
when I put you in the frame, I am not thinking of you.
Not of the trees that tower, of the wind that blows just now, Not any of it.

But of ten years, when this picture will be a memory
And I will show it to our kids,
to show of time gone by
Or show it to a friend,
to show a man who gave me heartbreak.
I am thinking of ten years later
Because that's the one way I know how to feel better,
And I might be standing here, next to you
But all I see is ten years later.

And all my present, Is only worth
If it will be a story told in the future
When I am somewhere better. Perhaps ten years later.
